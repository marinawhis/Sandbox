Inspired by r/Place on Reddit and homepage guestbooks from the 2000s, I thought it would be cool to offer something while you're here. Feel free to leave a little doodle down below! Or even your next masterpiece. Maybe share with your friends and draw together? Totally up to you.

how is it made?

This sandbox is based on the Dead Simple Grid by Vladimir Agafonkin.

It includes p5js library which is a JavaScript library and developed by the community but created by Lauren Lee McCarthy. It's made for creative coding and making JS more accessible for non-programmers.

The whiteboard is made with WBO. WBO is a free and open-source online collaborative whiteboard that allows many users to draw simultaneously on a large virtual board.
